Join Us!

Hey there! We are looking for graduate students to join our group. Read more here.

We welcome talented and enthusiastic students at any point in their education. Please contact Jed Brubaker for information about on-going projects or to inquire about opportunities in the lab.

Prospective Graduate Students

We are an interdisciplinary team composed of and working with students from programs across CU's campus. Students interested in pursuing a graduate degree and working with us should consider any of the following outstanding programs at CU:

  • Information Science (Ph.D.)
  • Computer Science (Ph.D., Masters)
  • Communication (Ph.D.)
  • ATLAS Institute (Ph.D. and Masters in Creative Technology and Design)
